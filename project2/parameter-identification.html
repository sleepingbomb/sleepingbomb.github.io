Parameter Identification

Experimental characterization of motor dynamics, spring stiffness, damping coefficients, and friction parameters for accurate simulation.

Overview

Accurate simulation requires precise knowledge of physical parameters. This section documents three key experiments conducted to identify critical system parameters for our grasshopper-inspired jumping robot.

Each experiment was designed and executed by a team member, focusing on a specific aspect of the mechanical system.
